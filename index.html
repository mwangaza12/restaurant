Our Story

A Culinary Journey Since 2010

Savory Heights was born from a passion for exceptional food and hospitality. Our journey began in 2010 when Chef Michael Raymond, after years of training in renowned kitchens across Europe, decided to bring his culinary expertise to create a unique dining experience.

Today, we pride ourselves on combining traditional techniques with modern innovation to create dishes that are both familiar and surprising. Our commitment to using locally-sourced, sustainable ingredients ensures that every plate not only tastes extraordinary but also supports our community.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a meal with loved ones, we strive to make every visit to Savory Heights memorable.
